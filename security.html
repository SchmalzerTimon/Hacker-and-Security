Security

Hacker also work for computer-security companies. They deal with security-mechanisms and their weaknesses.

A scriptkiddie is not the same as a hacker. If you want to read about them click here: Scriptkiddie

There are three different types of Hacker

  • white hats

    White hats test how safe the project is. They only hack in legal ways and work for companies.

  • grey hats

    Grey hats are not identified as "good" or "bad".

    They sometimes violate the hacker-ethics but they do it to reach a higher level.

    They try to publish security-vulnerabilities to stop deniers and to force the responsibles to remedy the vulnerabilities.

  • black hats

    Black hats work with kriminal energy to execute the assignment they got from the government or from a organization.

    If someone says you are a black hat, they refere to your type of hacking and not to you personally.

  • etymology

    The names are from old western films where the heros wore white hats, that is why the legal hackers are called white hats.
